About

MiCorr is a new and innovative application to study and analyse the microstructures and corrosion forms of ancient and historic metals.

The project is a joint between the faculty of conservation and restauration and the faculty of economics from the Haute école Arc in Neuchâtel, Switzerland. The project aims at developing a new and innovative way to analyse corrosion forms of ancient and historic metals based on their stratigraphies. The application will offer new tools to design, compare and search objects or forms of corrosion based on their stratigraphies.

Full text search

MiCorr gives you full text search access to all MIFAC-Metal objects. Get instant access to detailled descriptions.

Create Stratigraphies

Using MiCorr, you can create your own stratigraphies and search corresponding corrosion scheme.

Collaborate

With MiCorr you can share your work, get advices and find people working on the same type of objects.
